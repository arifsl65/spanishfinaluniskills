Our Story

With roots in Sarajevo and raised in Spain, I bring an authentic multicultural perspective to education. My journey began with qualifying as a Primary teacher in Modern Foreign Languages (English) at the Universidad de Valladolid and later on obtaining the QTS for England, followed by earning distinction at a prestigious British Council bilingual school in Soria.

For over 15 years, I've dedicated myself to excellence in London's diverse educational landscape, currently serving as a class tutor in the Special Educational Needs department at a West London college.

Our Approach

My teaching philosophy emerges from this unique international background combined with extensive experience in the British educational system. I believe that effective learning happens when instruction is personalized, culturally rich, and adaptable to individual needs.

Whether working with language learners, students with special educational needs, or professionals seeking to enhance their skills, I create environments where every learner can thrive.
